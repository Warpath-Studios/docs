**Does this require UMI?**  
No. Only UMI Interaction is required.

**Do foliage instances automatically become actors?**  
No. Only Complex types convert into actors.

**Can I integrate a custom loot system?**  
Yes. Override harvest logic in the actor.

**Does this work with World Partition?**  
Yes, the system was designed with streaming levels in mind.
